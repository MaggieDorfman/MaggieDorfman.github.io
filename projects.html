Devised Theatre to the Poems of Caitlin Windsor

As part of my Honors Capstone Thesis for Chapman University, I am currently working on directing, producing, and acting in a devised theatre piece to the poems of my best friend Caitlin Windsor. The poems reflect on her experience losing her father to Alzheimers last year, both before and after his passing. The piece of theatre will act as an incubator for my Capstone's theoretical implementation of Drops technology into non-profit theatres across the country. My hope is that this technology will allow patrons to choose to donate their spare change back into the theatre or to a charity associated with the cause they are watching on-stage in cases of shows like Rent or Angels in America or ect..

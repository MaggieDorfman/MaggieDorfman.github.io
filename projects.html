Wondering What I'm Up To?

Celebrating the Start of a New Chapter

This past May I sobbed as I submitted my final paper and graduated with a BFA in Theatre Performance and a minor in Chapman University’s Honors Program….and then I went to DISNEY WORLD for Toy Story Land!! I don’t know what was more exciting…being handed my diploma or entering the gates of Magic Kingdom.

After several weeks of family fun in New York, I started the next chapter of my life post-college and moved to Los Angeles to start the grind of adulthood. Check me out at R+D Kitchen in Santa Monica or in various audition rooms throughout Los Angeles! Cheers to what awaits!

Trip to Hong Kong

Post graduation I journeyed to Hong Kong for the EOS Hackathon. It was a crazy week full of new experiences and navigating sight seeing during a week full of typhoon warnings. And even though we didn’t place and couldn’t go to Disneyland Hong Kong it was one of the greatest experiences Ive ever had! Its definitely motivated me to learn how to code more and put exploring more of Asia on my bucket list!

Auditioning and then Some

Not only am I constantly auditioning for different projects, whether in person or via self tape, Im getting cast as well! Im super excited to be filming an independent short film called ‘Eyes On the Broad’, directed by Gabi Barrera-Poole, as well as 'Open Season', directed by John Hanle, this month!

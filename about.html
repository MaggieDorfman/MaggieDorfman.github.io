About Me

Who is Maggie?

The Real Me

I am full of opposites. I'm funny but serious; I'm sassy but polite; I'm a 5' 6" ball of spunk equally ready to sit down and watch netflix or travel the world. And, I approach my work the way I try to approach my life--by embracing impulses and accepting my contradictions.

Born and raised in the suburbs just outside NYC, I first garnered a love for acting after seeing Wicked on Broadway. Ever since, I have nurtured my passion and recently recieved a BFA in Theatre Performance from Chapman University, with a minor in the Honors College, in pursuit of that.

I am an intellectually driven and passionate actor ready to impact the world one project at a time. Send me a message to get to know me--the hot chocolate is on me!

This photo is from a Big Smiles Paint Day. I gave the kids from the Big Smiles Program at the UCLA Children's Hospital free range to splatter paint our founder, David Maliglowka's, t-shirt!

What Else Do I Do...?

Giving back to charity has always been an extremely important part of my life. In the third grade I started fundraising for JDRF, asking all the teachers in my middle school for donations and selling handmade bracelets at lunch, and ever since I have looked for any way to give back.

With this passion, I am extremely lucky to be a team member of Drops, a startup aimed at seamlessly integrating donating your spare change from everyday credit and debit card purchases to charity. Currently we are partnered with 20 charities and counting. It is extremely fulfilling to be able to not only help charities at a much larger scale than I ever imagined, but to be able to help bring the new generation of millennials into the field of giving back.
